蛛眼放大功能

Spider Eye magnification

    蜘蛛的视力较差，蜘蛛的脑袋顶上有许多小小的类似透镜的东西，这些就是它们的眼睛。
    蜘蛛的“透镜”可以让蜘蛛看清光线的明暗变化，但是无法让蜘蛛看清楚物体的形状，所以蜘蛛的视线是比较模糊。
    而这种凹透镜具有放大功能，所以蜘蛛眼中的世界可能是放大而模糊的。
Spiders have poor eyesight. There are many small lens like things on the top of their heads. These are their eyes.
Spider's "lens" can let the spider see the light and dark changes, but can't let the spider see the shape of the object, so the spider's line of sight is relatively fuzzy.
The concave lens has the function of magnifying, so the world in spider's eyes may be enlarged and blurred.

凸透镜成像与“幻觉”

Convex lens imaging and hallucination

    在放大镜所呈现出的影像的位置，在上面放置空白纸张，是无法成像的，没有确切的光线落在其上，但是把眼睛放在本该成像的位置，是可以看到模糊或清晰的像，这本身就是一种幻觉。
    而作为以凸透镜为眼睛的蜘蛛，以人的方式理解它，就是活在幻觉中的生物。以它的方式观察人，人也是活在幻觉中的。如果用蜘蛛大脑的后处理系统，转译人类的肉眼所见，就是光的数次折射所造成的结果。 
In the position of the image presented by the magnifying glass, it is impossible to place blank paper on it. There is no exact light falling on it. However, if the eye is placed at the position where it should be imaged, the blurred or clear image can be seen. This is an illusion in itself.
As a spider with convex lens as its eyes, it is a living creature living in illusion when it is understood in a human way. If we observe people in its way, people are also living in illusions. If we use the post-processing system of spider brain to translate what we see with the naked eye, it is the result of several refractions of light.

蜘蛛“大脑后处理”

Spider brain post processing

   蜘蛛的眼睛就是8个凸透镜。人们或许会认为它的视力很差，但是它大脑中对图像的处理是与人类不同的。
   视觉清晰度受限的蜘蛛、鱼和其他动物事实上也许比这项研究所表明的视力要好一些，因为它们的大脑具有“后处理”功能。
   我们可以用蜘蛛大脑成像的方式来转译人类肉眼所见的图像。
The spider's eyes are eight convex lenses. People may think it has poor vision, but the processing of images in its brain is different from that of humans.
Spiders, fish and other animals with limited visual acuity may actually have better vision than this study suggests, because their brains have "post-processing" functions.
We can use spider brain imaging to translate images seen by the human naked eye.
