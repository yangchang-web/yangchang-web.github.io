视觉简述

Visual description

捕鸟蛛眼部位于头胸甲顶部的凹陷处，很小，共8个，分两排排列。大部分不过是用于感知光线明暗、物体移动。其没有复眼，只有单眼。单眼是仅能感觉光的强弱，而不能看到物像的一种比较简单的光感受器。

捕鸟蛛前方两只眼睛是它们的固定“透镜”，其中微小的回飞棒形状的视网膜能够帮助跳蜘蛛接收到高分辨率的景象，并且同时还能看到不同的颜色以及紫外线。每只“首眼”内的视网膜都位于蜘蛛头内一条很长且可移动的管状体后面。蜘蛛可以移动这些管状体，以便四处观看。

有一位研究人员把蜘蛛看世界的方式比喻为用一个手电筒在四处“扫视”。从整体来讲，其他眼睛应该称为“副眼”，但是它们依然都有着很好的视觉，而且特别善于探测到移动物体。

The eye of the spider is located in the depression on the top of the carapace. It is very small, with 8 eyes arranged in two rows. Most of them are just used to sense the light and the movement of objects. There is no compound eye, only one eye. Monocular is a relatively simple photoreceptor which can only sense the intensity of light, but can not see the image.

The two eyes in front of the spiders are their fixed "lenses", in which the tiny fly back stick shaped retina helps the jumping spiders to receive high-resolution images, as well as to see different colors and ultraviolet rays. The retina in each "first eye" is located behind a long, movable tube in the spider's head. Spiders can move these tubes so they can look around.

One researcher likened the way spiders see the world to "scanning" around with a flashlight. On the whole, other eyes should be called "secondary eyes", but they still have good vision and are particularly good at detecting moving objects.
